Focus & Outcomes by phase

Phase 1 — Build Round Window: Q4 2025 • Target Completion: Q4 2025
  • Window: Q4 2025 (Oct–Dec)
  • Target Completion: Q4 2025
  • Objective
  • Lay the legal, structural, and technical groundwork for LumiLedger—protecting IP while advancing toward a secure, market-ready release as a compliant and scalable financial data platform.
  • Key Workstreams
  • Legal & Compliance Foundation: Engage counsel to finalize corporate structure; file copyrights and trademarks; and draft NDAs, partner agreements, privacy policy, terms of service, and data-use agreements for integrations (e.g., Plaid, Stripe).
  • Operational Readiness: Create disclosure and collaboration frameworks to safely work with external professionals during development.
  • Engineering & Technical Validation: Once NDAs are in place, bring in experienced engineers to review architecture, security, data handling, and performance; remediate blind spots; and harden the codebase for reliability and scalability.
  • Funding Use
  • Finance the legal, compliance, and early engineering refinement necessary to safeguard LumiLedger’s technology, brand, and data systems and to move confidently into broader engineering work.
  • Outcome: A protected, compliant, and technically validated foundation—ready for Phase 2 scaling and external pilot deployments.
Phase 2 — Scale Round Window: Q1 2026 • Target Completion: Q1 2026
  • Window: Q1 2026 (Jan–Mar)
  • Target Completion: Q1 2026
  • Objective
  • Activate go-to-market and user acquisition: heavier marketing, grassroots networking, and live user demoing—while enabling subscriptions and refining onboarding.
  • Key Workstreams
  • Marketing & Awareness: Run targeted campaigns, content, and events; build community through grassroots networking and advisor circles.
  • Live Demoing & Pilot Cohorts: Conduct hands-on demos and office-hour walkthroughs; establish early-adopter cohorts for feedback and references.
  • Onboarding & Activation: Streamline signup flows, data linking (e.g., Plaid), and first-week guidance; tighten success ops and support.
  • Subscriptions & Billing: Switch on paid subscriptions; implement billing operations and dunning; tune pricing/packaging based on live signals.
  • Scale Readiness: Continue stability/performance hardening and analytics instrumentation for growth.
  • Funding Use
  • Finance go-to-market execution—including campaigns, demo programs, community outreach, and onboarding improvements—plus the subscription/billing enablement required to begin monetization.
  • Outcome: Early-thousands active users, paid subscriptions live, repeatable demo-to-activation motions, and a stable system prepared for broader national expansion in Phase 3.
Phase 3 — Expansion Round Window: Q2 2026 • Target Completion: Q2 2026
  • Window: Q2 2026 (Apr–Jun)
  • Target Completion: Q2 2026
  • Objective
  • Execute the official go-to-market launch: subscription-required access, full operational cadence, and clear growth markers for marketing and revenue.
  • Key Workstreams
  • Launch & Distribution: Roll out public availability with coordinated campaigns, partner announcements, and advisor-channel amplification.
  • Operations at Scale: Run support and success operations daily; monitor SLAs, error budgets, and incident response; tighten onboarding SLAs.
  • Revenue Systems: Enforce subscription-required access; refine pricing & packaging; build dashboards for MRR/ARR, churn, and payback.
  • Partner Ecosystem: Formalize advisor/CPA partners and referral programs; expand integrations that drive adoption and retention.
  • Product Growth: Ship high-impact enhancements (analytics/KPIs, integrations, mobile) based on live user feedback and cohort analysis.
  • Funding Use
  • Provide early operations support—covering launch execution, customer success, infrastructure and reliability work, and the initial growth activities required to establish consistent revenue momentum.
  • Outcome: Official launch completed with subscription-required usage, operations in full swing, and marketing/revenue KPIs trending upward—positioning LumiLedger for sustained national growth beyond Phase 3.
