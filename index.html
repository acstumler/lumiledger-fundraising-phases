Funding Plan: three phases to reach scale.

Phase 1 — Build Round
Legal foundation followed by engineering hardening to advance a compliant and scalable financial data platform. Target completion: Q4 2025.
Phase 2 — Scale Round
Make the system reliable for heavier use, reach 1,000–3,000 active business users, turn on paid subscriptions, and hire a small team. Target completion: Q1 2026.
Phase 3 — Expansion Round
Grow nationally with marketing and advisor partners, expand the team, and set up 12–18 months of operating runway. Target completion: Q2 2026.

Focus & Outcomes by phase

Phase 1 — Build Round Window: Q4 2025 • Target Completion: Q4 2025
  • Window: Q4 2025 (Oct–Dec)
  • Target Completion: Q4 2025
  • Objective
  • Lay the legal, structural, and technical groundwork for LumiLedger—protecting IP while advancing toward a secure, market-ready release as a compliant and scalable financial data platform.
  • Key Workstreams
  • Legal & Compliance Foundation: Engage counsel to finalize corporate structure; file copyrights and trademarks; and draft NDAs, partner agreements, privacy policy, terms of service, and data-use agreements for integrations (e.g., Plaid, Stripe).
  • Operational Readiness: Create disclosure and collaboration frameworks to safely work with external professionals during development.
  • Engineering & Technical Validation: Once NDAs are in place, bring in experienced engineers to review architecture, security, data handling, and performance; remediate blind spots; and harden the codebase for reliability and scalability.
  • Funding Use
  • Finance the legal, compliance, and early engineering refinement necessary to safeguard LumiLedger’s technology, brand, and data systems and to move confidently into broader engineering work.
  • Outcome: A protected, compliant, and technically validated foundation—ready for Phase 2 scaling and external pilot deployments.
Phase 2 — Scale Round Window: Q1 2026 • Target Completion: Q1 2026
  • Window: Q1 2026 (Jan–Mar)
  • Target Completion: Q1 2026
  • Objectives
  • Make the system reliable and smooth for heavier use.
  • Reach 1,000–3,000 active business users.
  • Turn on paid subscriptions.
  • Hire 1 engineer and 1 growth/marketing role.
  • Key Workstreams
  • Stability and performance hardening.
  • Subscription enablement and billing operations.
  • Growth experiments and early partner channels.
  • Deliverable: 1,000–3,000 active business users, first paid subscriptions, and a reliable system by March 2026.
  • Capital type: Convertible/recoverable grants; non-dilutive accelerators
Phase 3 — Expansion Round Window: Q2 2026 • Target Completion: Q2 2026
  • Window: Q2 2026 (Apr–Jun)
  • Target Completion: Q2 2026
  • Objectives
  • National marketing and advisor partnerships.
  • Team expansion: finance/ops and customer success.
  • 12–18 months operating runway for payroll and vendors.
  • Advanced KPIs, integrations, and mobile.
  • Key Workstreams
  • Advisor channel build-out and partner marketing.
  • Team growth and success operations.
  • Product expansion: analytics, integrations, mobile footprint.
  • Deliverable: 10,000+ users, profitable operations within reach, and readiness for a Series A round by June 2026.
  • Capital type: Equity (SAFE/note) & strategic capital

Timeline

End of Q4 2025

Legal and compliance foundation complete, NDAs active, and engineering hardening underway for a market-ready build.

End of Q1 2026

First full version finished, subscriptions enabled, and pilot users onboarded into a stable system.

End of Q2 2026

Early-thousands active users, national GTM in motion, advisor channels building, team and features expanding.
